About JE Tech Hub Nigeria

JE Tech Hub officially launched in February 2024, marking a new phase of growth and innovation in Nigeria's tech education landscape. We deliver top-tier tech solutions while empowering Nigerian individuals and businesses with industry-leading skills.

With several training programs ongoing and approximately 30 graduates already making their mark in Nigeria's tech industry, we believe in nurturing creativity, fostering growth, and equipping our community with practical skills to thrive in the digital era.

What We Offer Nigerian Students

Hands-on training and innovative solutions tailored for the Nigerian market.

Operating under the umbrella of JE Gadgets, our parent brand specializing in gadget sales, repairs, and swaps across Nigeria. We pride ourselves on being a leading tech education center that empowers Nigerians to transform their ideas into impactful opportunities.

Whether you're a Nigerian entrepreneur looking to scale, a tech enthusiast eager to explore new frontiers, or someone seeking to upskill for the Nigerian job market, we offer the knowledge, tools, and mentorship to help you thrive.

Our Cutting-Edge Programs for Nigerian Students

We provide practical experience and expert guidance across various tech-driven fields. Our ongoing training programs are designed specifically for the Nigerian market and empower you with the skills needed to excel in Africa's growing tech ecosystem.

Software Development

Learn to build responsive websites and mobile apps using modern technologies. Perfect for Nigeria's growing fintech and e-commerce sectors.

Video Editing and Graphics Design

Master video editing and graphic design for Nigeria's booming entertainment industry and digital marketing sector.

Data Analysis

Turn data into insights for Nigerian businesses using Excel, Python, SQL, and Power BI. High demand in banking and telecommunications.

Forex Trading

Learn to trade global financial markets with focus on Naira-based strategies and African market opportunities.

UI/UX Design

Gadget Repairs & Sales

Learn smartphone and computer repairs, plus sales strategies for Nigeria's growing gadget market. Start your own tech business.
